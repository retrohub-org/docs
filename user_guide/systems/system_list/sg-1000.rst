Sega SG-1000
============

Basic Information
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
- **Short name:** ``sg-1000``
- **Type:** Console
- **Supported extensions:** 12
	- .rom
	- .ri
	- .mx1
	- .mx2
	- .col
	- .dsk
	- .cas
	- .sg
	- .sc
	- .m3u
	- .bin
	- .zip
